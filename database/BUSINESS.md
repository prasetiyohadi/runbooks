## 11. Common Optimization Strategies

### Index Best Practices
- **Primary Key Indexes**: Always create on uniquely identifying columns
- **Foreign Key Indexes**: Essential for JOIN operations
- **Composite Indexes**: Multi-column indexes for WHERE conditions spanning multiple columns
- **Full-Text Indexes**: Enable fast text search on large documents
- **Partial Indexes**: Index subsets of rows for further optimization

### Monitoring & Maintenance
- **Query Profiling**: Use EXPLAIN/ANALYZE to identify slow queries
- **Index Statistics**: Keep statistics updated (enables optimal query plans)
- **Unused Index Cleanup**: Remove unused indexes (reduce INSERT/UPDATE overhead)
- **Continuous Improvement**: Regular review and optimization prevents regressions

## 12. Risk Mitigation

### Common Concerns & Solutions

**Concern**: "Indexing slows down inserts/updates"
- **Solution**: Proper index design minimizes overhead; benefits outweigh costs
- **Strategy**: Essential indexes only; remove redundant indexes
- **Result**: Net positive performance (100x read speedup vs 10% write slowdown)

**Concern**: "Complex to manage indexes"
- **Solution**: Automation tools (pg_stat_statements, MySQL optimizer hints) simplify management
- **Barrier to Entry**: Simple indexes easy; complex optimization requires expertise

**Concern**: "Risk of wrong indexes"
- **Solution**: Test on staging first; monitor performance after deployment
- **Validation**: EXPLAIN output clearly shows improvement
